Surf schools

The surf school that i always use is called Narosa surf school and is based in Donegal Ireland. Its a really great school and they have lots of differnent kinds of lessons you can get. For example, you can do a single 90 minute lesson or you can do a surf camp where you surf every day for a week. The instructors are all really nice and friendly and if you're nervous they're really good at keeping you calm. Another surf school is Coast To Coast school that is based in Dunbar, Scotland. It was founded in 2004 and they also teach paddle boarding and coasteering as well as surfing. Theres also Outer Reef surf school in Pembrokeshire, Wales.
