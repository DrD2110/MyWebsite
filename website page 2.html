My favourite surf spot

One of my altime favourite surf spots is Falcarrah Beach in Co. Donegal, Ireland. Its the perfect place to go if you're looking to go for a nice relaxing surf as the water is really calm in between sets and its a very secluded, peaceful beach. Its also a great beach for beginners because the waves arent to big and the sets are quite small.
